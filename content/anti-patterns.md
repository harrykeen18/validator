# Customer Discovery Anti-Patterns

## 1. Confirmation Bias
Hearing what you want to hear. Recording "that's kind of annoying" as "VALIDATED."

## 2. The Solution Interview
Pitching your solution then asking "would you use this?" Everyone says yes to be polite.

## 3. The Expert Interview
Asking for industry opinions instead of personal experiences.

## 4. The Survey Shortcut
500 shallow surveys instead of 10 deep conversations.

## 5. Friends & Family
Validating with people who want you to succeed.

## 6. Building While Validating
Writing code before talking to customers.

## 7. Vanity Metrics
Counting landing page signups as validation.

## 8. Pivot Avoidance
Ignoring evidence that contradicts your hypothesis.

## 9. Analysis Paralysis
Doing interview #30 instead of making a decision.

## 10. Feature Request Trap
Building what customers say they want vs. what they actually need.
