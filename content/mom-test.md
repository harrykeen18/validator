# The Mom Test — Quick Reference

By Rob Fitzpatrick

## Three Rules
1. Talk about their life instead of your idea
2. Ask about specifics in the past instead of generics or opinions about the future
3. Talk less and listen more

## The Test
If you asked your mom about your business idea, she'd say it's great — because she loves you, not because it's a good idea. The Mom Test is about asking questions that even your mom can't lie to you about.

## Key Techniques
- Ask about what they already do, not what they would do
- Ask about the last time they encountered the problem
- Ask what they've already tried
- Ask where the money comes from
- Get concrete specifics, not vague generalities
